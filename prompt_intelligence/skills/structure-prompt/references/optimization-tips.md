# Token Optimization Tips

- **Lead with the task** — put the core instruction in the first two sentences.
- **Use lists for constraints** — easier to scan than prose paragraphs.
- **One role, one job** — split multi-purpose prompts into separate prompts when possible.
- **Negative constraints sparingly** — prefer "Do X" over long "Do not …" lists.
- **Format by example** — a short output example often replaces paragraphs of format description.
- **Cut pleasantries** — omit "please", "I would like", and meta-commentary about being an AI.
